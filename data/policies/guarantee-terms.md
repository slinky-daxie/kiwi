# Pineapple Travel Guarantee Terms

## Coverage Overview

The Pineapple Travel Guarantee protects customers booking self-transfer itineraries (virtual interline) against common disruption scenarios.

## What's Covered

### Missed Connections
- **Short layovers (<2 hours)**: FULLY COVERED
- **Reasonable layovers (2-4 hours)**: COVERED if delay was unavoidable
- **Long layovers (>4 hours)**: Customer responsible unless extraordinary circumstances

### Flight Delays
- **Minor delays (<1 hour)**: Not covered
- **Significant delays (1-3 hours)**: Rebooking assistance provided
- **Major delays (>3 hours)**: Full rebooking or refund offered

### Cancellations
- **Airline cancellations**: FULLY COVERED - rebooking or full refund
- **Customer-initiated**: Not covered unless within 24hr booking window
- **Weather-related**: Covered if customer purchased guarantee

### Schedule Changes
- **Minor changes (<2 hours)**: Customer notified, no action needed
- **Major changes (>2 hours)**: Rebooking options provided at no cost
- **Route changes**: Full refund or alternative routing offered

## What's NOT Covered

- Customer no-shows
- Missed connections due to customer delays (late check-in, shopping, etc.)
- Changes to visa requirements
- Strikes and labor disputes (unless customer purchased premium guarantee)
- Acts of war or terrorism
- Customer-initiated changes after 24hr window

## Geographic Coverage

- **Worldwide**: All routes covered
- **EU Routes**: Additional EU261 rights apply (see separate policy)
- **US Routes**: DOT passenger rights apply

## Eligibility

- Must be purchased at time of booking
- Applies to entire itinerary (cannot purchase per segment)
- Valid for one-way and round-trip bookings
- Maximum trip duration: 30 days

## Claims Process

1. Contact Pineapple support immediately when disruption occurs
2. Provide booking reference and flight details
3. Agent will assess coverage and propose resolution
4. Resolution executed within 24 hours (or immediately for in-airport emergencies)

## Resolution Options (at Pineapple's discretion)

- Rebooking on next available flight (same or alternative route)
- Full refund of affected segments
- Compensation voucher for future travel
- Hotel accommodation if overnight stay required
- Ground transportation to alternative airport

## Cost

- Standard Guarantee: £15-50 depending on route complexity
- Premium Guarantee: £50-100 (includes strikes, political events)
